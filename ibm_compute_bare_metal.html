Synopsis¶

Create, update or destroy an IBM Cloud ‘ibm_compute_bare_metal’ resource

Requirements¶

The below requirements are needed on the host that executes this module.

  • IBM-Cloud terraform-provider-ibm v1.2.1

  • Terraform v0.12.20
